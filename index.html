“Design a typeface inspired by a character from your favourite book, movie or tv series. Your typeface should visually reflect the personality of your chosen character.”

This hand Rendered typface was inspired by the character Stefan Salvatore, from the Novel and tv series “The Vampire Diaries.” The idea of the oval shaped letters and numbers comes from the “daylight ring” that Stefan Salvatore wears. This is a very important
part of the character because without it, he cannot survive. I used the colours red, black, and white. The Red represents the blood, the Black is his dark side, and the White, his good soft side. I used a sort of old style with sharp edges demonstrating danger.
